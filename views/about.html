Why use the Ambit Intervals Designer?

The Suunto Ambit range are great multisport watches, however the support for structured workouts is limited compared to competing watches. The built-in interval timer only allows you to specify high and low intervals based on distance or time. You cannot specify a target (pace, heart rate, cadence), and each interval needs to be of the same duration.

The built-in interval timer will not guide you through a structured run session such as:

  • 15min Warm Up
  • 1200m @ 04:00 pace (400m RI)
  • 4x400m @ cadance > 170 (200m RI)
  • Cool Down
The Ambit Interval App Generator allows you to design your own interval sets.

Suunto Ambit Apps

One of the cool features of the Ambit watches is the ability to create custom apps for the watch. The apps can do anything from calculating how many beers you have burned off during your exercise, to guiding you through some high intensity intervals.

So even if the built-in interval timer is limited, structured workouts can be implemented as custom apps. There is an existing Interval App Generator that lets you specify a set of intervals with different durations (based on time or distance), but it does not let you specify a target for each interval (pace, heart rate, cadence). This is something this generator adds.
